Why Rep

Everything your training needs

Science-based workout planning

Tell Rep your goals, equipment, and schedule. It builds a periodised program — Push/Pull/Legs, Upper/Lower, or Full Body — using established sports science principles, with auto-scheduled deload weeks so you never overtrain.

Track every set, rep & weight

Log exercises with RPE, warmup flags, and rest timers. Full history means Rep always knows your last working weight and suggests the right progression.

Recovery & readiness score

Rep reads sleep, resting heart rate, and body weight from Apple Health or Google Health Connect — entirely on-device — and flags when to push hard or back off.

Honest calorie tracking

Active calories from your wearable via Apple Health or Health Connect. No wearable? A MET-based estimate keeps things sensible. Stored locally — never uploaded.

Built different

Powerful by design. Simple to use.

Progressive overload

Rep suggests weight increases based on your actual history. No guesswork — smart defaults you can override any time.

Auto-deload weeks

After 3–4 weeks of training, Rep automatically schedules a lighter deload week and cycles your split to keep you progressing.

Privacy first

Your workout data lives on your device. Health data (sleep, HRV, body weight) is never uploaded anywhere. You control what syncs.

6 beautiful themes

Midnight Indigo, Forest Noir, Crimson Ember, Cream Sage, Fog & Slate, and Sand & Clay. Match your vibe.

Fully offline

Everything works without internet. Your data lives in a local database on your device and syncs to the cloud when you're back online.

Social feed

Share workouts, follow other athletes, and stay accountable together. See what your friends are lifting in real time.
